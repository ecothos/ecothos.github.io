About

Ecothos Fisheries Consultants,LLC is an independent team exploring data-based scentific solutions to support sustainable fisheries within the ecosystem itself. Our team of experienced fisheries data professionals is dedicated to working collaboratively with our clients to design and implement data collection programs that generate high-quality,comprehensive datasets. We believe in employing rigorous data management practices, using the latest technologies and analytical tools, and applying statistical and modeling approaches to extract meaningful insights from complex datasets. Through our work, we aim to support evidence-based decision making in fisheries management,promote transparency and accountability, and foster collaboration among stakeholders. We are committed to staying at the forefront of developments in data science and technology, and to continuously improving the quality and utility of our services.

Our mission is to provide reliable data collection, management, and analysis services to support sustainable fisheries management and conservation

Our vision is to be a leading provider of innovative and effective data solutions that promote the long-term sustainability of aquatic resources and support the livelihoods of fishing communities worldwide.

Our way is to leverage our expertise and commitment to sustainability, integrity, collaboration, innovation, and excellence to provide reliable data solutions that enable evidence-based decision-making, promote responsible fishing practices, and support the long-term health and productivity of aquatic ecosystems and the communities that depend on them.
